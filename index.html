Neolithic

New game difficulty:

The intended experience.

How to play: Drag to pan, pinch to zoom, tap to select. Tap a build button then a tile to place. Construction and repairs need a villager — an idle one is sent automatically, or tap a villager then the site. Tap a villager, then a forest / hill / fertile tile to gather. Train defenders at the Barracks to fight wolves and raiders.

Win: stockpile 4000 resources with 20 population, or destroy the rival Town Center, or survive to day 120 (~20 minutes). Lose: your Town Center falls — or the rival prospers first.
