📖 How to play

Basics: Drag to pan, pinch to zoom, tap to select. Tap a build button then a tile to place. Construction and repairs need a villager — an idle one is sent automatically, or tap a villager then the site. Tap a villager, then a forest / hill / fertile tile to gather — tiles run dry and turn to stumps or pebbles, so build farms, camps and quarries for steady income. Train defenders at the Barracks to fight wolves and barbarians.

Win: destroy the rival tribe's Town Center. Lose: yours falls. Nothing else ends the game — build up, then go get them.

Farms, lodges, lumber camps and quarries only produce while a villager is stationed there. Every valley is short on one resource — scout for it and hold it.
